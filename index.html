Our Complete Policy Platform

Foundational Principle: Principled Australian Sovereignty in a Multipolar World

Key Policy Areas:

  • Foreign Policy & Defence: Realist engagement with all major powers, including pragmatic relations with China and Russia, focused solely on Australian interests
  • Nuclear Energy: Lift the moratorium and pursue Small Modular Reactors (SMRs) for reliable baseload power
  • COVID-19 Accountability: Royal Commission and Truth & Reconciliation process to investigate pandemic response
  • Housing & Infrastructure: Fast rail connecting regional hubs, "First Home Buyer Plus" scheme with shared equity
  • Constitutional Reform: Entrench freedom of speech, increase political transparency, protect sovereignty
  • Law & Justice: Mandatory sentences for corruption, prison reform focused on rehabilitation
  • Tax Reform: Phased simplification toward flatter tax system, "One-In, Two-Out" red tape reduction
  • Energy Security: "All-of-the-above" strategy including nuclear, gas, and sensible renewables
  • Emissions Reduction: Best-effort approach that prioritizes energy reliability and economic growth
